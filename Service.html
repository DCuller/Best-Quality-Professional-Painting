Interior Painting

When painting the interior of your home, our professional team of painters will always take great care to protect your home and belongings using plastic, masking paper and clean interior drop cloths. The surfaces will be properly prepared before applying paint, and most importantly, we clean up everything when we're finished, leaving your home spotless.

  • Walls & Ceilings
  • Plaster & Drywall
  • Doors & Windows
  • Kitchen Cabinets
  • Baseboard, Crown and Any Trim Mouldings
  • Wood Handrails & Stairways

Exterior Painting

Our team will treat the exterior of your home like we do your interior: with great care. Whether you have a sprawling mansion or simple cottage-like home, Professional Painters guarantees the highest level of craftsmanship. All surfaces will be prepared the right way before applying any paint!

  • Doors & Windows
  • Decks, Railings and Patio Covers
  • Shutters
  • Gutters and Downspouts
  • Siding: Composite, Wood & Vinyl
  • Stucco & Brick
